Wild Crowns

Role: Solo Developer | Engine: Unity6 | Year: 2025 | Project Status: Ongoing


About the Game

Wild Crowns is a top-down autobattler / roguelike game. Think of it as if TFT but PVE.
In Wildcrowns, you hire mercanaries defend your kingdom from waves of enemies. Each Mercanaries has a race (feline, canine, rodent, reptile, etc.) and a class (warrior, archer, mage, etc.) that activates certain synergies. You can also build structures to help you in battle or increase your resource income.
