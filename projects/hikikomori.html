Hikikomori Simulator

Role: Solo Developer | Engine: Unity6 | Year: 2025 | Project Status: Abandoned


About the Game

Hikikomori Simulator is a semi-idle game where you play as a fresh graduate hikikomori, a person who withdraws from social life. Do remote jobs, buy foods online, collect nerdy figurines, and study online courses to get a faster job promotion. Just don't forget to manage your mental health! to eat, drink, sleep, and pay your rent on time.
